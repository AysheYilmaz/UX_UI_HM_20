BarFly
BarFly is a group project. My role is UX researcher and UI Designer

Tools
Google Survey, interviews, FigJam, Figma

Problem
As a user researcher I want to understand professionals bar outings and the decisions that lead up to to choosing certain places. How might we optimize their experience to make a more efficient decision to save time on choosing where?
Solution
BarFly makes ‘I wonders’ into a guarantee with the ability to access live wait times and add your party size to be next without the surprise at the door.
Process
Collecting live user data through interviews and surveys while creating and iterating a product that meets their needs over a 4 week period
Research
As a user researcher I want to understand professionals bar outings and the decisions that lead up to to choosing certain places. How might we optimize their experience to make a more efficient decision to save time on choosing where?
Research Process
We want to understand why people go out late and what determines where they go about for bars/hangouts. We utilized google surveys, 1:1 interviews and user persona.
